31 /**
32 * @brief Represents a hierarchical SceneGraph that organizes all renderable
33 * and non-renderable objects within a scene.
34 *
35 * Every scene has one implicit root node all nodes are appended to when
36 * `addNode` is called.
37 *
38 * A Scene is responsible for managing the SceneGraph, consisting of SceneNodes;
39 * it provides methods to frustum cull SceneNodes based on a specific `FrustumCullingStrategy`
40 * and a particular `CameraSceneNode` and also allows for propagating world transformations
41 * through the contained subtrees.
42 *
43 * @see [RTR, pp. 828]
44 * @see [Gre19, pp. 693]
45 * @see [HDMS+14, pp. 138]
46 *
47 * @note By default, we're not allowing copying/moving Scenes.
48 * These constraints may be relaxed in the future, depending on valid use cases.
49 */
